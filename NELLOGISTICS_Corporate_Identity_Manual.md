NELLOGISTICS exists to **eliminate uncertainty in global supply chains** by delivering data-driven, technology-enabled cargo logistics solutions that empower businesses to move faster, smarter, and with complete transparency across every mile of their international shipments.

NELLOGISTICS embodies:
- **Tech-Forward Innovation**: We lead with cutting-edge tracking systems, predictive analytics, and automation

- **Speed & Precision**: High-volume operations executed with accuracy and urgency

### Brand Promise
*"Your cargo, our data. Delivered with precision."*

NELLOGISTICS promises to transform traditional logistics into a seamless digital experience where clients have:
- **24/7 Real-Time Tracking**: Know exactly where your shipment is, down to the GPS coordinates
- **Predictive ETA Intelligence**: AI-powered arrival predictions with 98% accuracy
- **Automated Compliance**: Smart documentation that handles customs, regulations, and certifications
- **Instant Issue Resolution**: Problems detected and resolved before they impact delivery
- **Carbon Footprint Transparency**: Track and optimize environmental impact of every shipment

### Typography

#### Primary Font Family: **Inter**

**Rationale:** Inter is a carefully crafted, highly legible variable font designed specifically for digital interfaces. Its geometric yet humanist structure provides exceptional clarity at all sizes, perfect for data-dense logistics dashboards and real-time tracking interfaces.

**Font Weights:**
- **Light (300)**: Supporting text, data labels
- **Regular (400)**: Body text, descriptions
- **Medium (500)**: Navigation, buttons
- **Semibold (600)**: Subheadings, card titles
- **Bold (700)**: Main headings, section titles
- **Extrabold (800)**: Hero text, key metrics

**Character Spacing:**
- Headlines: -0.02em (tight)
- Body text: 0em (normal)
- UI elements: 0.01em (slightly open for legibility)

**Line Height:**
- Headlines: 1.1
- Body text: 1.6
- UI elements: 1.4

#### Monospace Font: **JetBrains Mono**
**Usage:** Tracking numbers, container IDs, reference codes, timestamps
**Rationale:** Designed for code, provides exceptional clarity for alphanumeric tracking identifiers

## III. Communication and Content Guidelines (English)

### Tone of Voice

#### Core Principles

**1. Tech-Forward**
- Lead with data, metrics, and technology capabilities
- Use precise technical terminology without jargon
- Emphasize automation and AI-driven solutions

**Example:**
- ❌ "We'll keep you updated on your shipment"
- ✅ "Automated GPS tracking updates every 15 minutes with predictive ETA calculations"

**2. Proactive**
- Communicate solutions before clients know there's a problem
- Frame everything in terms of prevention and optimization
- Use active voice and action-oriented language

**Example:**
- ❌ "If there's a delay, we'll let you know"
- ✅ "Our AI detected potential port congestion 72 hours ahead—we've already rerouted to maintain your delivery window"

**3. Transparent**
- Share all relevant data openly
- Explain the "why" behind every decision
- Provide visibility into processes and pricing

**Example:**
- ❌ "Additional fees may apply"
- ✅ "Fuel surcharge: $45 (based on current Brent crude at $82/barrel, updated daily)"

#### Writing Style

**Headlines:**
- Short, action-driven, metric-focused
- Start with verbs when possible
- Include specific data points

**Body Copy:**
- Concise, scannable paragraphs (3-4 lines max)
- Use bullet points for complex information
- Lead with the most critical information

**Data Presentation:**
- Always include units (kg, km, hours, USD)
- Use real-time timestamps
- Provide context for all metrics

---

### Imagery Style

#### Primary Visual Direction: **Digital Process Visualization**

**What to Use:**
- Abstract data flow diagrams with glowing connections
- Clean, minimalist 3D renderings of container movement
- Dashboard interfaces showing real-time tracking
- Geometric patterns representing global networks
- Heat maps and data visualization overlays
- Modern warehouse automation (robotics, conveyor systems)
- Satellite and aerial views with data overlays

**What to Avoid:**
- Traditional cargo ship photography
- Generic port/warehouse exteriors
- Handshake/business meeting stock photos
- Cluttered or busy compositions
- Outdated technology imagery

#### Image Treatment

**Color Grading:**
- Cool tones (blues, teals) dominant
- Selective use of Velocity Green for focal points
- High contrast with deep shadows
- Slightly desaturated for modern aesthetic

**Composition:**
- Clean negative space
- Geometric balance
- Visual flow from left to right (movement/progress)
- Layered depth with foreground/background separation

## IV. Digital Platform Guidelines (English)

### Web Performance Standards

**Page Load Requirements:**
- First Contentful Paint: <1.2 seconds
- Largest Contentful Paint: <2.0 seconds
- Time to Interactive: <3.0 seconds
- Cumulative Layout Shift: <0.1

**Optimization Strategies:**
- Implement lazy loading for tracking maps
- Use WebP/AVIF formats for imagery
- Minimize JavaScript bundle size (<150kb gzipped)
- Server-side rendering for critical path content

---

### SEO Guidelines

**Keyword Strategy:**
- Primary: "international cargo tracking", "real-time logistics", "AI-powered shipping"
- Secondary: "container tracking technology", "predictive logistics", "automated freight"

**Content Structure:**
- H1: Include primary keyword + location
- Meta descriptions: 150-160 characters with tracking/technology focus
- Alt text: Describe visual + include relevant logistics terms

---

### Automation & User Experience

**Chatbot Personality:**
- Name: "FLOW" (Freight Logistics Operations Workflow)
- Tone: Efficient, helpful, data-driven
- Response style: Immediate answers with tracking numbers/timestamps

**Email Automation:**
- Real-time shipment alerts with GPS coordinates
- Proactive delay notifications with alternative solutions
- Weekly performance metrics (on-time %, average transit time)

**Dashboard Defaults:**
- Show most critical data first (active shipments, delays, ETAs)
- Auto-refresh every 30 seconds
- Customizable widgets based on user role

## III. Directrices de Comunicación y Contenido (Español)

### Tono de Voz

#### Principios Fundamentales

**1. Tecnológicamente Avanzado**
- Liderar con datos, métricas y capacidades tecnológicas
- Usar terminología técnica precisa sin jerga
- Enfatizar automatización y soluciones impulsadas por IA

**Ejemplo:**
- ❌ "Le mantendremos informado sobre su envío"
- ✅ "Rastreo GPS automatizado con actualizaciones cada 15 minutos y cálculos predictivos de ETA"

**2. Proactivo**
- Comunicar soluciones antes de que los clientes sepan que hay un problema
- Enmarcar todo en términos de prevención y optimización
- Usar voz activa y lenguaje orientado a la acción

**Ejemplo:**
- ❌ "Si hay un retraso, le avisaremos"
- ✅ "Nuestra IA detectó congestión portuaria potencial 72 horas antes—ya hemos redirigido para mantener su ventana de entrega"

**3. Transparente**
- Compartir todos los datos relevantes abiertamente
- Explicar el "por qué" detrás de cada decisión
- Proporcionar visibilidad sobre procesos y precios

**Ejemplo:**
- ❌ "Pueden aplicarse tarifas adicionales"
- ✅ "Recargo por combustible: $45 (basado en Brent actual a $82/barril, actualizado diariamente)"

#### Estilo de Escritura

**Titulares:**
- Cortos, orientados a la acción, enfocados en métricas
- Comenzar con verbos cuando sea posible
- Incluir puntos de datos específicos

**Texto de Cuerpo:**
- Párrafos concisos y escaneables (máx. 3-4 líneas)
- Usar viñetas para información compleja
- Comenzar con la información más crítica

**Presentación de Datos:**
- Incluir siempre unidades (kg, km, horas, USD)
- Usar marcas de tiempo en tiempo real
- Proporcionar contexto para todas las métricas

---

### Estilo de Imágenes

#### Dirección Visual Principal: **Visualización de Procesos Digitales**

**Qué Usar:**
- Diagramas abstractos de flujo de datos con conexiones luminosas
- Renderizados 3D limpios y minimalistas de movimiento de contenedores
- Interfaces de panel mostrando rastreo en tiempo real
- Patrones geométricos representando redes globales
- Mapas de calor y superposiciones de visualización de datos
- Automatización de almacenes moderna (robótica, sistemas de transporte)
- Vistas satelitales y aéreas con superposiciones de datos

**Qué Evitar:**
- Fotografía tradicional de buques de carga
- Exteriores genéricos de puertos/almacenes
- Fotos de archivo de apretones de manos/reuniones de negocios
- Composiciones desordenadas o ocupadas
- Imágenes de tecnología obsoleta

#### Tratamiento de Imagen

**Gradación de Color:**
- Tonos fríos (azules, teals) dominantes
- Uso selectivo de Verde Velocidad para puntos focales
- Alto contraste con sombras profundas
- Ligeramente desaturado para estética moderna

**Composición:**
- Espacio negativo limpio
- Balance geométrico
- Flujo visual de izquierda a derecha (movimiento/progreso)
- Profundidad en capas con separación primer plano/fondo
